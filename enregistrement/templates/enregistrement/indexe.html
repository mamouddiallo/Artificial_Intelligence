About Int. _ Art.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines programmed to think, learn, and make decisions. It powers applications like voice assistants, autonomous vehicles, and advanced data analytics. AI uses technologies such as machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision to perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence. Its potential spans industries, from improving healthcare diagnoses to optimizing business operations, while raising important ethical and societal considerations about its impact on jobs, privacy, and decision-making.
